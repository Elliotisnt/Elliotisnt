## Hi, I'm Elliot

Statistics major at Purdue Fort Wayne. I work on NSF-funded research applying SINDy (Sparse Identification of Nonlinear Dynamics) to discover governing equations in dynamical systems. Our first paper was recently published in [*Nonlinear Dynamics, Psychology, and Life Sciences*](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41410560/). Interested in applied biostatistics, epidemiology, and public health.

Outside of research, I like building things that solve problems I have: a [macro indicator monitor](https://github.com/elliotisnt/bretton-woods-decay) for retirement allocation decisions, a [Pi-hole DNS server](https://github.com/elliotisnt/dietpi-pihole) on a repurposed school laptop, and a containerized media stack on a a 2010 Mac Pro I rescued from a recycling drive at my dad's office.
